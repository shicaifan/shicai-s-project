Call for Special Sessions

Interested attendees are encouraged to propose special sessions/workshops, each of which consists of five papers providing a focused discussion of new or innovative topics. Each proposal must include the session/workshop title, description, organizers and affiliations. Proposals should be sent to the workshop chairs or special session chairs with the following emails.
Special Session Chairs E-mail: zhi.wei@njit.edu; shuaili@polyu.edu.hk; cc to icnsc2018@qq.com and icnsc2018@jnu.edu.cn
Workshop Chairs E-mail: ac.ammari@yahoo.fr; qkang@tongji.edu.cn; cc to icnsc2018@qq.com and icnsc2018@jnu.edu.cn
Special session/Workshop papers will undergo the same intense review process and submission is not a guarantee for acceptance. Special session/Workshop papers must be clearly indicated when submitted with a copy sent to the designated special session/workshop organizer.
