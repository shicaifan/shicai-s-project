Theme: Towards an intelligent world

The 2019 IEEE International Conference on Networking, Sensing and Control will be held in Banff, a national park in Alberta, Canada. This conference brings together both academy and industry to address new challenges, share solutions and discuss future research directions. It will feature plenary speeches, panel sessions, tutorials, workshops, interactive sessions, and invited/special sessions. Contributions are expected from academia, industrial, and management agencies. The conference theme is: Towards an intelligent world.
