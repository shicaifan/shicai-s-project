Dr. Zofia Lukszo, is full professor at the Delft University of Technology, the Netherlands. She heads the Energy and Industry Group at the Faculty on Technology, Policy and Management. Her research concentrates on a wide range of problems in the way complex energy systems are functioning and can be (re-)shaped for the sustainable future. Shaping smart grids, which require additional flexibility from the users in interaction with dispatchable power plants, storage and interconnections, forms a main pillar in her research. As a senior IEEE Member she contributed to many international workshops and conferences. She has over 150 refereed scientific publications and book contributions.

Research Interests

  • Modelling and optimization of energy systems
  • Electric mobility
  • Agent-based modelling
